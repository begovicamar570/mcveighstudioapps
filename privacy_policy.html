Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 17, 2025

Introduction

McVeigh Studio ("we", "our", "us") built the Sleep Monitor · Snore Recorder app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app and what measures we take to protect your privacy.

Company: McVeigh Studio
Address: Wehntalerstrasse 574, 8046 Zürich

Key points — short summary

  • We operate offline. Your recordings and analysis stay on your device.
  • We do not require account sign-up and we do not collect or share personal data with third parties.
  • The app is fully ad-free and contains no trackers.

Information the app may access

To provide Sleep Monitor's core functionality the app may access device features and create local files. Below is what we may access and why:

  • Microphone / Audio Recording: to record snoring and nighttime sounds. Recordings are stored locally on your device unless you explicitly export or share them.
  • Local Storage: to save recordings, sleep reports, and soundscapes for offline use and faster performance.
  • Optional Media Files: if you import or download ambient soundscapes, those files are stored locally to allow offline playback.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not upload your recordings or personal data to our servers by default.
  • We do not require an account, email address, or phone number to use core features.
  • We do not display ads, embed third‑party analytics, or include tracking SDKs.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data to advertisers or data brokers.

User choices & control

You control how recordings are stored, exported, or deleted. Typical controls include:

  • Export or share a recording manually (e.g., to email or another app) — this action is initiated by you and will use your chosen sharing method.
  • Delete recordings or reset local data from within the app at any time.
  • Revoke microphone permissions in your device settings to stop new recordings; previously saved files remain on device until you remove them.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect local data stored by the app. Because files reside on your device, we recommend using your device's lock screen, passcode, or full‑disk encryption features to secure access. We cannot access or recover files stored locally unless you choose to share them with us.

Children

Our app is not directed to children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal data to the app, please contact us at the address below.

Third‑party services

The core app does not integrate third‑party analytics, advertising networks, or remote storage. If a future update adds optional third‑party features (for example, a cloud backup you opt into), those features will be described clearly and will require your explicit consent.

Data retention

Recordings and reports are kept on your device until you manually delete them. If you uninstall the app some platforms may remove the app's local files automatically; we recommend exporting important files before uninstalling.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. If we make material changes that affect how we treat existing data or introduce new remote data processing, we will notify you within the app (and update the effective date at the top of this page).
